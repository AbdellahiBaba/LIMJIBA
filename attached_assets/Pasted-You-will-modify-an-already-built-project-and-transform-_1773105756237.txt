You will modify an already‑built project and transform it from a plastic‑industry management system into a full E‑commerce management system.  
Your task is to update the existing codebase, add new modules, and integrate the following features exactly as described:

====================================================
🔄 1) Add Shipping Cost After Purchase Registration
====================================================

Current issue:
The system only allows entering shipping cost at the moment of purchase.  
But in real workflows, the shipping invoice often arrives days later.

Required modifications:

- Add the ability to attach a shipping cost to an existing purchase invoice at any time.
- Automatically distribute the shipping cost across all products in that purchase using one of two methods:
  - Distribution by quantity
  - Distribution by product value
- Recalculate and update the **actual unit cost** after adding shipping.

Example:
Purchased 100 units at 10 each → total 1000  
Shipping invoice arrives later → 200  
New total = 1200  
New unit cost = 12

====================================================
📊 2) Profitability per Product & per Purchase Batch
====================================================

Current issue:
The same product may be sold at different prices (retail, wholesale, discounts, offers).  
The system must calculate profit based on the actual cost of each purchase batch.

Required modifications:

- Track profitability per purchase batch.
- Track profitability per sale transaction.
- Track remaining profit in stock.

Example:
Purchase batch:
100 units at cost 12

Sales:
30 units at 20  
40 units at 18  
30 units at 15  

System must show:
- Total profit for this batch
- Profit per sale
- Profit remaining in inventory

====================================================
🚚 3) Add Customer Delivery Cost to Sales Invoice
====================================================

Required modifications:

- Add a field named “Delivery Cost” to sales invoices.
- Delivery cost must be added to the invoice total.
- Delivery cost must appear in:
  - Invoice view
  - Reports
  - Profit calculations

Example:
Products total = 100  
Delivery = 5  
Invoice total = 105

====================================================
📈 4) Required Reports
====================================================

Add reporting modules for:

- Product profitability
- Profitability per purchase batch
- True product cost (purchase + shipping)
- Profit by date range

====================================================
🛒 5) Convert the Entire System to E‑commerce Context
====================================================

Modify the existing project so it becomes an E‑commerce management system instead of a plastic‑industry system.  
This includes:

- Updating terminology (products, customers, orders, delivery, stock, etc.)
- Ensuring all new features integrate smoothly with the existing architecture
- Maintaining clean, modular code
- Ensuring all calculations update automatically across the system

====================================================
🧩 Final Deliverables
====================================================

- Updated codebase with all new features implemented.
- Clear documentation explaining:
  - New modules
  - Updated database fields
  - How shipping distribution works
  - How profitability is calculated
- Ensure backward compatibility with existing data where possible.

Proceed with implementing all modifications now.
